Day 25

6h time difference strikes.

This morning, I got to see the fun result of three classes in six weeks combined with a 6 hour time difference in the class that I'm TAing for. A flurry of work in essays and exams over the last few days meant that I needed to really remember that this is a several-week marathon.

Teaching assistants aren't paid enough. More broadly, university student positions don't pay enough and it's exploitation. What really gets me is that the people I work under seem to agree, but student employment policy has extremely low caps.
